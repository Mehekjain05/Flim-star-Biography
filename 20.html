Who is Mandy Takhar ?

Mandy Takhar is a British actress of Indian origin who works in Indian cinema - predominantly Punjabi with roles in Hindi and Tamil films as well.

Mandy Takhar was born and raised in the city of Wolverhampton in the UK.[1] She traces her roots to Maliana, a small village near Phagwara in Punjab, India.[2] She moved to London from her family home when she was 17, to study drama at the Kingston University and then left the UK in 2009 to work as an actor in the Indian film industry
