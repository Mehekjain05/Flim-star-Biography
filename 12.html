Who is Meera mithun ?

Meera Mitun (born Tamizhselvi)in 1984 is an Indian model and actress, who has appeared in Tamil language movies. After beginning her career as a model, she became an actress, making her debut in the movie 8 Thottakkal (2017). [1][2]Meera is known for her extensive controversies and allegations.

Meera Mitun was born as Tamizh Selvi.in 1984.[3] She is from Chetpet and is the daughter of D. Mani, a civil engineer and Shyamala, a homemaker. Her brother Thamizhamuthan is an engineer.[4] She did her bachelor's degree in microbiology from Ethiraj College for Women and her master's degree from SRM University Coimbatore.[5] She then briefly worked as a researcher at Dr Cherian's Foundation.[4][6] Mitun is divorced following a brief marriage which lasted for very few days
