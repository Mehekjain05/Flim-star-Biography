Who is Candice Warner ?

Candice Warner is a retired Australian professional ironwoman and surf life saver. Warner is of Maltese heritage. She is the wife of Australian cricketer David Warner since 2015. They had their first child (a daughter), Ivy Mae on 11 September 2014 and a second daughter, Indi Rae on 14 January 2016 and a third daughter Isla Rose born on 30 June 2019. They live in Sydney.

Candice Warner was born on 13th March 1985 (age 36 years as in 2021) in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia. Her father's name is Michael Falzon, and her mother's name is Kerry Falzon. She has a sister named Emerald Kelly and a brother named Mathais Kelly.
