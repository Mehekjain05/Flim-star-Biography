Who is Guri ?

Guri is an Indian [5] Punjabi Singer. Guri got fame from his songs like Yaar Beli], Dooriyan, Mil Lo Na, Jimmy Choo Choo and Bewafa Tu. Guri's all songs released on 26th day of month. From 2017, Guri is signed with Geet MP3 .

Guri began his singing debut in Song Mashooq Fatte Chakn in 2016 with SMI Audio. He became famous in very short days with his outstanding Singing performance to his audience. After this song he signed with Geet MP3. Geet MP3 released his 5 songs in 2017 and 9 songs in 2018. Guri also sung 3 songs without videos named Hanju, Teri Meri, Jaan.
