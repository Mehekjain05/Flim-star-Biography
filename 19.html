Who is Harish Verma ?

Harish Verma is an Indian film actor who mostly works in Punjabi-language films. He made his debut in the 2010 film Panjaban, and gained wide attention with Yaar Annmulle (2011). He made his singing debut with the track Ikk Vaari Hor Soch Lae, which was written by Jaani and produced by B Praak.

Harish Verma is an Indian actor as well as model. He is est known for playing the role of 'Jatt Tinka' in the Punjabi film 'Yaar Anmulle.'
